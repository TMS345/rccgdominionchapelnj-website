Who Are We?

This is a faith community whose mission is spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. Founded in 1999, we have remained a vibrant and inclusive congregation that strives to impact our community and beyond positively.

If you are looking for a place to grow in your faith, connect with God and others, and make a positive impact in the world, we invite you to join us at Dominion Chapel, regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey.

Our Services

Sunday Service

Be a part of our worship services every Sunday at 12 PM, where we gather to praise, reflect, and grow in the faith together.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to grow in faith and leadership through fun, engaging activities and weekly lesson. Our goal is to help youth build a strong foundation in Christ while developing lasting life-skills.

Bible Study

Dive deeper into the Word with our Bible study sessions, held every Tuesday at 7 PM via Zoom. For insightful discussions, spiritual growth, and community connection, click this link to join: Join via Zoom

Prayer Meetings

Start your day with spiritual strength and upliftment at our weekly prayer meetings, held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 5 AM. Join us in prayer from the comfort of your home by dialing +1 563 999 1485.

Night Vigil

Join us for an inspiring night of worship, reflection, and spiritual renewal at our Night Vigil Services, held every last Friday of the month at 11 PM via telephone. Dial in using the phone code: +1 563 999 1485.

Sunday School

Experience the joy of learning and growing in faith at our Sunday School every Sunday at 11 AM. Dive into engaging lessons, connect with a welcoming community, and enrich your spiritual journey. Whether you're new to faith or looking to deepen your understanding, our Sunday School offers something for everyone.
