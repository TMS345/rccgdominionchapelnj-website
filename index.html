WHO ARE WE?

This is a faith community dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. We are a vibrant and inclusive congregation located in Galloway, NJ, and we strive to make a positive impact in our community and beyond.

If you are looking for a place to grow in your faith, connect with God and others, and make a positive impact in the world, we invite you to join us at Dominion Chapel. Regardless of where you are on your spiritual journey, you are welcome here. We look forward to getting to know you and walking alongside you on your faith journey.

OUR SERVICES

Sunday Service

Be a part of our worship services every Sunday at 12 PM, where we gather to praise, reflect, and grow in the faith together. Whether you're new to the area or seeking a spiritual home, we invite you. We look forward to seeing you there!

Bible Study

Dive deeper into the Word with our interactive Bible study sessions, held every Tuesday at 7 PM. No matter where you are, you can join us via Zoom for insightful discussions, spiritual growth, and community connection. You can click this link to join: Join via Zoom

Weekly Prayer Meetings

Start your day with spiritual strength and upliftment at our weekly prayer meetings, held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 5 AM. Join us in prayer from the comfort of your home by dialing +1 563 999 1485. We look forward to connecting with you!

Night Vigil

Join us for an inspiring night of worship, reflection, and spiritual renewal at our Night Vigil Services, held every last Friday of the month at 11 PM via telephone. Dial in using the phone code: +1 563 999 1485. We look forward to seeing you!

Sunday School

Experience the joy of learning and growing in faith at our Sunday School every Sunday at 11 AM. Dive into engaging lessons, connect with a welcoming community, and enrich your spiritual journey. Whether you're new to faith or looking to deepen your understanding, our Sunday School offers something for everyone. We can't wait to see you there!
